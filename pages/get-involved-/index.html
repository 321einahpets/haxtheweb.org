University / Organization that wants to get involved?

Great! Here's some of the best ways for organizations to try "HAX" and get involved in the project.

  • Reach out to any of the members of the core project team or who's listed at the bottom of the FAQ. We're happy to do conference calls to forge partnerships especially around HAX as a Learning management or OER production solution though it has implications for any public site!
  • Try out the HAXcms live demo (this website has no backend)
  • Reach out in in the HAXcms issue queue with questions
  • Bring this website to developers in your organization or other code junkies and tell them to explore

HAX makes up multiple projects across hundreds of web components and various other repos. It can be a bit overwhelming but I want you to imagine something... Imagine being a developer on HAX.

Because all it takes to get involved in HAX is, that's right, showing up in our repos and getting involved! Yes you too can be just as special and meme-able as we are!
